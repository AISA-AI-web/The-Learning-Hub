Athletics & Activities

LZ

Luka Zischka

Athletics & Activities Director

lzischka@aisa.sch.ae

Lion Cubs (Grade 2–6)

Junior swimmers from Grade 2; Grade 4–6 compete in the Junior Emirates Athletic Conference (JEAC) across three seasons: soccer, track & field, and basketball. No-cuts, equal playing-time philosophy.

Senior Lions (Grade 6–12)

Middle School, JV, and Varsity teams compete in EAC, ISAC, and internationally in NESAC. Seasons cover swimming, volleyball, tennis, soccer, basketball, cross-country, band, choir, track, badminton, and academic games.
