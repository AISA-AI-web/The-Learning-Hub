School

What is the technology like in the classrooms?

All classrooms have Apple TV and projectors. Teachers receive Apple MacBooks or iPads depending on subject and grade. Wireless access is school-wide. Students in Grades 1–12 bring their own Apple devices — iPad in Grades 2–5, Apple laptop in secondary. KG and Grades 1–5 have a weekly tech class in their own labs. KG shares five iPad carts that teachers check out.

Are middle school classrooms shared between teachers?

Secondary teachers have their own classrooms (with some exceptions), shared with specialist teachers (Arabic, Islamic, PE, etc.).

Where can I find information about the curriculum?

The AISA curriculum lives on the school's internal network. Reach out to Sarah Layman, our Curriculum Coordinator, for information ahead of arrival.

Should I ship teaching materials (posters, resources, etc.)?

There's a teacher store in Abu Dhabi with a decent selection — only a little more expensive than in the US. Bring favourites you can't replace, but don't go crazy. The school is well-resourced (apart from commercially-produced posters). It may be cheaper to bring an extra suitcase than to ship.

Housing

Where is the teacher housing located?

Most new teachers are housed in Muroor, Reem, and Khalidiya. Each area has restaurants, cafes, and a grocery store within walking distance.

What size beds are in the school apartments?

Queen-sized beds in school-provided accommodation.

Will there be bedding when I arrive?

Yes — a small welcome pack with basic bedding to get by until you can shop. Nothing fancy, just the essentials.

What kind of plugs are in the apartments?

Three-prong UK outlets. Adapters work for most US devices and are easily available locally (including 5–6 outlet power strips). The UAE runs 240V — the US runs 120V — so for some items (electric razors, curling irons, hair straighteners) you may need a converter rather than just an adapter.

What's already in the furnished housing?

Apartments contain slightly different items because previous teachers may leave bonus pieces. Generally expect:

  • Living / dining: sofa set, coffee table / end tables, lamp(s), dining table and chairs
  • Bedroom: queen bed, wardrobe(s), chest of drawers, night table
  • Kitchen: stove, oven, refrigerator, washing machine, basic cookware
